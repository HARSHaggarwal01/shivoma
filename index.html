Discover Krishna Bhoomi: A Divine Journey through the Land of Lord Krishna and Goddess Radha

Embark on a spiritual odyssey to the enchanting realms of Krishna Bhoomi, where the divine love of Lord Krishna and Goddess Radha resonates through sacred lands. Our meticulously curated tour invites you to immerse yourself in the divine essence of Vrindavan, Mathura, Gokul, Govardhan, and Barsana. Each destination, steeped in mythological significance and spiritual heritage, promises to transport you to a realm where divinity and devotion converge.

Vrindavan: The Playground of Divine Love
  • Mathura: The Birthplace of Lord Krishna
  • Gokul: The Abode of Childhood Miracles
  • Govardhan: The Hill of Divine Protection
  • Barsana: The Land of Goddess Radha

Jabalpur to Mathura Round Trip.

Morning:

Arrival in Mathura: Arrive at Mathura Railway Station and transfer to Vrindavan. Bihari Ji Temple: Start your spiritual journey by experiencing the enchanting darshan of Lord Krishna at the iconic Bihari Ji Temple. Radha Vallabh Temple: Visit this serene temple dedicated to Radha and feel the divine presence.

Afternoon:

Radha Raman Temple: Marvel at the self-manifested deity of Lord Krishna. Spend time exploring the spiritual energy of this ancient temple.

Evening:

ISKCON Temple: Participate in the vibrant evening aarti at the famous ISKCON (Krishna-Balaram Mandir) and immerse yourself in the devotional chanting.

Dinner: Return to your accommodation for a comforting dinner and reflect on the day’s spiritual experiences.

Overnight stay in Vrindavan.

Morning:

Nidhivan: Begin your day exploring the mystical forest where Krishna performed Ras Leela. The unique atmosphere of Nidhivan adds a divine charm to the place. Keshi Ghat: Visit the sacred ghats along the Yamuna River, where Krishna performed various divine acts. Enjoy the peaceful surroundings and the historical significance.

Afternoon:

Prem Mandir: Visit the stunning Prem Mandir during the day to admire its architecture and spiritual aura. Walk through the beautiful gardens and explore the carvings depicting Krishna’s life.

Evening:

Vaishno Devi Temple: Seek blessings at this unique shrine that blends the devotion to Vaishno Devi with Krishna’s divine energy.

Prem Mandir Light Show: Return in the evening to witness the spectacular light and sound

show at Prem Mandir, a visual treat with devotional music.

Dinner: Enjoy a delicious dinner at your accommodation and relax.

Morning:

Transfer from Vrindavan to Mathura Start your journey with an early morning transfer from Vrindavan to Mathura, taking approximately 30 minutes. Witness the sacred land where Lord Krishna was born and lived his early life.

Krishna Janmabhoomi Temple

Begin your spiritual tour by visiting Krishna Janmabhoomi Temple, the birthplace of Lord Krishna. Spend time absorbing the sacred energy of this revered site, exploring the temple’s deep historical and spiritual significance.

Dwarkadheesh Mandir

Proceed to the famous Dwarkadheesh Temple, an architectural masterpiece dedicated to Lord Krishna. Admire the temple’s vibrant carvings and tranquil environment as you connect to its divine essence.

Mid-Morning:
Mathura Ghats
Take a peaceful stroll along the serene ghats of Mathura. Visit the well-known Vishram Ghat, where it is believed Lord Krishna rested after killing the demon Kansa. Enjoy the scenic views of the Yamuna River, reflecting on the spiritual energy that flows through the area.

Lunch:
Lunch at a Local Vegetarian Restaurant
Enjoy a traditional vegetarian meal at a local restaurant. Savor local delicacies like kachori, aloo sabzi, and the famous Mathura peda.

Afternoon:
Visit the 84 Khamba Temple, known for its historical and spiritual significance. This unique temple is named after its 84 pillars, representing the 84 Mahantas of the region. Learn about the rich legacy of these saints as you admire the ancient architecture.

Brahmand Ghat
Explore Brahmand Ghat, the mystical site where young Krishna, playing with his friends, revealed the entire universe in his mouth to his mother, Yashoda. Spend some time meditating or simply reflecting on this divine miracle.

Late Afternoon:

Visit Raman Reti, where Krishna played during his childhood. Devotees believe walking barefoot on the sands here is an act of devotion, reconnecting with Krishna’s playful and divine energy.

Evening:
Cultural Event or Satsang
As the day winds down, attend a cultural event or a satsang (spiritual discourse) that delves into the life and teachings of Lord Krishna. Enjoy devotional music, classical dance performances, or spiritual talks that resonate with the energy of Mathura.

Return to Hotel and Dinner
After a spiritually enriching day, return to your hotel for a relaxing dinner. Reflect on the experiences of the day as you rest and prepare for the next part of your journey.

Morning:
Depart for Barsana
Begin your day early with a drive to Barsana, a serene town associated with the life of Radha Rani, the eternal consort of Lord Krishna. The drive from Mathura takes about an hour.

Shri Ji Mandir (Radha Rani Temple)
Start your journey with a visit to the beautiful Shri Ji Mandir, located atop the Bhanugarh hill. Dedicated to Radha Rani, this temple offers breathtaking views of Barsana. Take part in the morning aarti and feel the divine energy as you offer your prayers to Radha, the embodiment of pure love and devotion.

Nandbhavan (Nanda Maharaj’s House)
Visit Nandbhavan, the ancestral home of Nanda Maharaj, the father of Lord Krishna. Explore this sacred site where Krishna spent his childhood, and learn about the deep connection between the village and the divine Leelas (pastimes) of Krishna. The architecture and rustic charm of Nandbhavan give insight into the early life of Krishna.

Charan Pahadi
Head towards Charan Pahadi, where the divine footprints of Krishna and Radha are believed to be etched into the rocks. Take a moment to meditate and absorb the spiritual significance of this site, which marks the divine presence of the two deities.

Lunch:
Vegetarian Lunch at a Local Eatery
Pause for lunch at a nearby local restaurant. Enjoy a wholesome vegetarian meal with local delicacies like kachoris, sabzi, and yogurt-based dishes, perfect for refueling for the rest of the day.

Afternoon:
Pawan Sarovar
Visit Pawan Sarovar, a tranquil lake in the heart of Barsana. The serene surroundings make it an ideal place for meditation or quiet reflection. It's believed that Radha and Krishna spent time by this sacred water body, adding to its spiritual importance.

Gahwar Van
Explore Gahwar Van, a sacred forest where Radha and Krishna are said to have spent time together, lost in their divine love. Walk through the forest, reconnecting with nature and the stories of Krishna’s playful pastimes with the gopis. This enchanting forest is filled with peace and devotion, providing a perfect atmosphere for reflection.

Rawal Gaon (Radha Rani's Birthplace)
Visit Rawal Gaon, the birthplace of Radha Rani, located a short distance from Barsana. The temple here honors Radha’s divine birth, and the atmosphere is filled with devotion. Spend time exploring the temple and connecting with the stories of Radha’s early life.

Evening:
Return to Hotel
After a spiritually fulfilling day in Barsana, make your way back to your hotel in Mathura or Vrindavan. Enjoy a peaceful drive, reflecting on the divine love between Radha and Krishna that permeates the entire region.

Night:
Vegetarian Dinner at Your Hotel
Relish a delicious vegetarian dinner at your hotel, featuring local specialties and fresh, flavorful ingredients. Let the peaceful vibes of the day continue as you savor the meal.

Rest and Recharge
Retire early to rest after a day of spiritual exploration. Prepare for the journey ahead, allowing yourself time to relax and absorb the day’s spiritual energy.

Morning:
Head to Govardhan
Begin your day with an early morning drive to Govardhan, located about 30 minutes from Mathura. This sacred site is associated with Lord Krishna’s legendary act of lifting the Govardhan Hill to protect the villagers of Vrindavan from Indra’s wrath.

Govardhan Hill Parikrama
Perform the sacred Govardhan Parikrama (circumambulation) around Govardhan Hill. This 21 km spiritual walk is one of the holiest acts for devotees of Lord Krishna. For a shorter experience, you may do the half-parikrama which covers significant parts of the hill and lasts about 3 hours. Along the way, you’ll pass through numerous temples and sacred spots connected to Krishna’s divine Leelas.

Radha Kund and Shyam Kund
Visit Radha Kund, one of the holiest water bodies in India, associated with Radha and her divine love for Krishna. Adjacent to it is Shyam Kund, linked to Krishna. Spend time meditating by the serene waters, soaking in the spiritual energy that flows through these sacred sites.

Lunch:
Lunch at a Local Restaurant
Enjoy a traditional vegetarian meal at a local eatery near Govardhan. The food here is simple but full of flavor, with dishes like sabzi, dal, and rotis, providing the perfect fuel for your spiritual journey.

Afternoon:
Kusum Sarovar
Head to Kusum Sarovar, a beautifully serene lake surrounded by ornate temples. This peaceful spot is connected to the pastimes of Radha and Krishna, and it’s said Radha used to collect flowers here for Krishna. Marvel at the magnificent architecture of the surrounding buildings and the tranquil waters that reflect divine love.

Mansi Ganga
Explore Mansi Ganga, a sacred lake created by Krishna’s mind. This divine water body is central to Govardhan Parikrama and holds deep spiritual significance. Devotees believe that bathing in Mansi Ganga cleanses sins and brings them closer to Krishna. Spend time reflecting by the water or take a boat ride for a peaceful experience.

Dan Ghati
Visit Dan Ghati, the site where Krishna playfully collected taxes from the gopis as they passed through with their milk and butter. The temple here is a reminder of Krishna’s mischievous yet divine interactions with his beloved devotees. Listen to the stories of Krishna’s playful pastimes and enjoy the vibrant energy of the place.

Jatipura Mukarvind
End your Govardhan journey with a visit to Jatipura Mukarvind, a temple dedicated to Krishna’s victory over Lord Indra. It marks the spot where Krishna showed his divine form to the villagers after lifting Govardhan Hill. The temple is an important site of worship for devotees performing Parikrama.

Evening:
Transfer to Mathura Railway Station
After a spiritually enriching day in Govardhan, head back to Mathura Railway Station. The drive takes about 45 minutes, allowing you some time to relax and reflect on the day’s experiences.

Board Train from Mathura to Jabalpur
Arrive at Mathura Railway Station to board your train back to Jabalpur. As you embark on your overnight journey, carry with you the divine experiences of the day and the blessings of Radha and Krishna.

Overnight Journey: Travel back to Jabalpur, resting after a day of spiritual discovery.
